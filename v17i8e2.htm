Now in Kindle Format

Rusty has converted our newsletters into an e-book.

Rusty wrote to tell us that he has reformatted our newsletters into e-book format.

Hi,

Having enjoyed your work for a few years, I purely for my own selfish reasons took the liberty of converting all of your online columns (v1n1to v17n7) into e-book format to load up my new Kindle that I recently received as a gift.  If you'd like one, I'd be happy to e-mail it to you (the file, not the Kindle ).  I've got it in ePub, MOBI, and azw3 formats (the conversion process is a little unwieldy, I had to convert it to one format before converting it to Kindle format).  azw3 = 15.7 Mb, MOBI = 16.5 Mb, and ePub = 13.7 Mb.

Regards,
Rusty

I don't have a Kindle (or any other e-book reader), so the file would not be of any value to me. However, it might be of value to you, so I asked Rusty if he could make it available to the public. He replied,

Sure thing.  I cobbled together a little page to serve them up.  I guess if my hosting company gets swamped with traffic, I'll have to figure something else out, but here it is for now:

http://sae.webhop.net

Regards,
Rusty

This is a good opportunity to say (again) that we are pleased for anyone to reproduce and distribute our work, providing proper credit is given.

We keep hoping that someone will convert our "Wizard of Ooze" radio drama, or any of our song parodies, into videos and post them on YouTube where they would get much more exposure.

Somebody did post our 2-minute "Evolution for Intellectuals" video on YouTube, but they cut off the credit at the end. That was a little tacky--but we would rather have it posted without credit than not posted at all.
