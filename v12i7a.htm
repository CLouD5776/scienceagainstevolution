About Us - April 2008

Who Writes This Stuff?

Chris wrote:

I've been browsing some of your website, lately, but I cannot find anything about the author(s?) of the articles. Is there any way I can find out anything about the authors themselves? Maybe they give seminars or something?

As it says in the return address, this newsletter is edited by R. David Pogge (better known as Do-While Jones). The feature articles contain links in the upper-right corner that say, "by Do-While Jones." The underline indicates a link. The reader is expected to click on the link to find out about the author if he wants to. So, we were somewhat puzzled by Chris' inability to find out anything about the author.

When we clicked on the author link, we were surprised to find the link didn't work any more! It still works on our local server, directing the browser to http://www.ridgenet.net/~do_while/, but didn't work on the public server. The problem turned out to be a relative path that no longer works on the public server. (Our Internet service provider changed their equipment, which also caused our web page counter not to work for several weeks.) We will fix the author link on our future articles; but it is a daunting job to go back and change the link on all the other articles published in the past 12 years, so it might take a while.

Information about the officers is found at http://scienceagainstevolution.org/corp.htm#directors.

We hate to brag, so we don't. We don't rely on proof by intimidation. Don't believe us (or anyone else) because we (or they) say so. We want you to learn as much about the theory of evolution as you can, and draw your conclusions based on facts, not credentials.

The officers do give lectures and/or seminars from time to time in response to local invitations. Since no officer or member has ever been paid anything (not even for travel) at Science Against Evolution, out-of-town speaking engagements are rare. But, if you happen to live somewhere near a nice vacation spot, or near one of our children or grandchildren, then we might accept the invitation.

There is no set fee for lectures or seminars. We simply accept whatever donations are collected.
