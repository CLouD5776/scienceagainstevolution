The Wizard of Ooze on Stage

It has been our custom for the last several years to feature a parody in our April newsletter (because April First is National Evolution Day). Our April 2001 newsletter featured a parody called, The Wizard of Ooze.

We were pleased to read in the last ARK Foundation newsletter that The Wizard of Ooze was performed live at their March 10th meeting.
