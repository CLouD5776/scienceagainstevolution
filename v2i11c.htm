Calendar - August 1998

4th Annual Community Dinner

A Community Dinner will be held at the Desert Empire Fairgrounds, September 12th, from 1 PM - 8 PM to benefit local non-profit organizations, including Science Against Evolution. There will be food, entertainment, and each organization will have a display booth.

Everybody gets a good deal! When you buy a $10 ticket to the dinner, you not only get dinner (tri-tip, garlic bread, cole slaw, beans, and a beverage), you also get coupons redeemable at John's Pizza, Ridgecrest Cinemas, Blimpe, Nicoletti's, and Mediacom totaling more than $24. If you buy the tickets from Science Against Evolution, nine dollars of each ticket sold goes to Science Against Evolution.

Please buy your tickets (before September 10) from us by calling Dave or Susan at 375-3089.
