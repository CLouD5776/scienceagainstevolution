The Wizard of Ooze

Last Month's feature essay about the Earth-Moon System was pretty heavy. To balance that out, our April newsletter will feature a light-hearted parody in which Dorothy (at the suggestion of the Genie from the Center for Science Education) travels from Kansas down the double-helix road to see The Wizard of Ooze, to ask him how life began in a puddle of slime.

We will also take a more serious look at the current evolutionary thinking of how life began. Don't miss next month's newsletter!
