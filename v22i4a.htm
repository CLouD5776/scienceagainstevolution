Internet Censorship

AOL won't let us email you.

If you sent us an email from your AOL account and didn't get a response from us it is because AOL won't let us answer your question. AOL blocks all email originating from ScienceAginstEvolution.INFO. When we try to answer your question, we get this notification:

----- The following addresses had permanent fatal errors -----

(reason: 521 5.2.1 : AOL will not accept delivery of this message.)

AOL isn't the only Internet service provider that protects you from free speech. Hotmail blocks all email from KRSF Christian Radio (KRSF.NET). Every February, KRSF responds to Darwin Day by broadcasting radio dramas of our parodies, "Alice in Evolutionland" 1, "The Wizard of Ooze" 2, and other anti-evolution programs. Does Hotmail block KRSF emails because of the anti-evolution programming, or does it block KRSF because it is a conservatively Christian radio station, or is there another reason?

A few years ago someone infected some Internet browsers. If you tried to go to our website, a bogus warning would pop up asking you if you really wanted to risk going to our website, which supposedly contained dangerous malware. Our website has never contained any malware. The only thing dangerous on our website is the truth.

In recent years, some universities have created "safe spaces" that protect their delicate snowflakes from facts that discredit liberal ideology. The evidence against the theory of evolution is near the top of their list of dangerous ideas.

In 2017, there were violent riots at universities by liberal bullies on those few occasions when a university had the audacity to allow a conservative to speak.

We don't censor anybody. We give you links to what evolutionists say because we have the truth on our side. We trust you to listen to both sides and make the intelligent decision.
