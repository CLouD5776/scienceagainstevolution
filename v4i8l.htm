All the News that Didn't Fit - May 2000
by Do-While Jones

Late-Breaking Developments

We had to bump three columns from this issue at the last minute.
  1. The discovery of the fossil that some believe is a dinosaur heart.
  2. A review of the TV special "Walking with Dinosaurs" and Disney's movie "Dinosaur."
  3. Katlin's reaction to our "Lucy" essay.

Unfortunately, we discovered just before press time that we had incorrectly attributed some statements about radioactive dating to Tim Thompson, and we wanted to give him a chance to respond. So, we are holding those three columns for next month.
