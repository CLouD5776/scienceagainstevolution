About Us - September 2000

Why Pay? It's Free!

Science Against Evolution is a non-profit, secular, educational corporation supported by members who donate just $15 per year and get our newsletter mailed to them. But we allow members and non-members alike to copy our newsletter and distribute it. We post our articles on our web site, where anyone can read them without contributing a penny.

We don't charge admission, or even take up an offering, at our Fourth Friday Free Films. You can get all the information we provide for free. So, why pay for it?

Because your contributions allow us to give this information free to others.
