Grand Canyon Creation Tour (Cr)

Each year the Institute for Creation Research leads tours through the Grand Canyon, explaining how the geologic evidence in the Grand Canyon supports the creation model better than the evolution model. Actually, they offer three different tours at the same time. One is a raft trip. The second is a backpacking trip. The third is a bus trip. This year the tours will be

March 20 - 28.

For more information call Cindy Gabay, at the Institute for Creation Research. (619) 448-0900 x6034.
