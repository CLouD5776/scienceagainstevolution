Calendar - September 21-25, 2003

Evolution Exposé

Invite your friends to this free, five-night seminar, September 21 through 25. The seminar will be held by special arrangement with the Ridgcrest Seventh-day Adventist Church, at 7 PM, 555 W. Las Flores.

The nightly topics are:

  • Sept. 21 - Dirty Little Secrets
  • Sept. 22 - Chemistry Against Evolution
  • Sept. 23 - Geology Against Evolution
  • Sept. 24 - Biology Against Evolution
  • Sept. 25 - Engineering Against Evolution

The meetings will begin promptly at 7 PM (which is a half-hour earlier than our usual meeting time). We will try to hold the presentations to one hour, but realize that some people may want to stay later to "discuss" some of the material further.

We expect these meetings to be interesting, light-hearted, and educational. The presentation is designed to be at the high-school science level.
