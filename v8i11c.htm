Calendar - September 18, 2004

10th Community Dinner

We will be participating again this year at the 10th Annual Community Dinner on Saturday, September 18, from 1 PM - 8 PM. The Community Dinner benefits all Ridgecrest non-profit organizations who choose to participate. There will be food, entertainment, and each organization will have a display booth.

The theme for the booth (and the feature article in the September newsletter) will be Seven Mysteries of Evolution. We hope those of you who live in the Ridgecrest area will attend the dinner, bring some friends, visit the booth, and (of course) purchase your $10 tickets from us. Community Dinner ticket sales allow us to keep our annual membership dues to $15 per year.
