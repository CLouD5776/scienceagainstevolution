Science Against Evolution doesn't have a very big budget. We only spend about $2,000 a year. Nearly all of that goes for the cost of printing and mailing about 200 newsletters each month. The rest goes for advertising and the Fourth Friday Free Films. Nobody who works for Science Against Evolution is paid. It is an entirely volunteer, non-profit corporation.

But our $2,000 budget has to come from somewhere. Since we do not receive money from any church or other organization, that money has to come from people like you. We suggest an annual donation of $15 per year, but we gladly accept donations in any amount, larger or smaller.

Our membership list is confidential. We do not give or sell it to any person or organization. Your will not receive any material from anyone other than us. The only material you will receive from us is Disclosure, our monthly newsletter.

There are some people, however, who aren't afraid to take a position against the theory of evolution, have made special donations to support our activities, and have asked for public recognition of their support. We are glad to do this by bestowing the honorary title of "Fellow" on those people who have donated $50 or more in the past year. We give the honorary title of "Senior Fellow" to those people who have donated $100 or more in the past year.

We hope you will agree that it is worthwhile to maintain this web site, publish our newsletter and Swap Sheet advertisements, and present the Fourth Friday Free Films. If so, we hope that you will encourage us to continue our educational campaign by giving generously to Science Against Evolution. Please use this membership application .
