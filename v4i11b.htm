Behind the Scenes - August 2000

Our New Domain Name

We have had more than 10,000 visitors to our web site, despite our obscure Internet address. Now we have made our web site even easier to find. The old address still works, but now you can also find us at

www.ScienceAgainstEvolution.org

Note that the domain is ".org" (not ".com") because we are a non-profit corporation, rather than a commercial enterprise. You need not capitalize the S, A, and E. The capital letters just make the name easier to read.

In light of the recent concern about Java scripts that exploit security holes in Netscape browsers, we would like to point out that none of our web pages has ever used a Java script, applet, or cookie. (You can easily verify this by viewing the page sources.) We do not collect any information about the people who visit our site, nor do we leave any information on our guest's computers. You can visit our site with complete assurance that neither your privacy nor computer have been violated.

We keep graphics to a minimum, to make our pages download as fast as possible. We don't want to keep you waiting for a slow download. We know your time is valuable. You certainly have better things to do than wait for gratuitous graphics to load. And we don't annoy you with advertisements, either.

We update the web site a day or two after the fourth Friday of every month. We hope you will encourage your friends to visit it monthly.
