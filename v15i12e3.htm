Evolutionists' Greatest Fear

Eric's email illustrates why evolutionists don't want you to question the theory.

We received this email from a software engineer named Eric who was looking for articles about the Ada programming language. Since I have written more than 50 of them, and a book about Ada, and helped revise the language, it isn't surprising that he found me. He wasn't looking for anything about evolution.

Dear Do-While,

I am diving into Ada, partly out of need to expand my software background and partly for possible, future employment.  I found your web site while Googling for Ada programming magazines.  I've seen your pen name before in other literature and Ada web sites.

Anyways, I was excited to see your anti-evolution web site!  I was once an ardent supporter of evolution until I began to ponder the utter complexity and beauty of nature. This started me down a road that led me to challenge the authenticity of the Bible. After much debate and being shown much evidence to support the claims of the Bible, including the testimony of the disciples and the Apostle Paul, I eventually took that "leap of faith" and accepted Jesus Christ as my Savior and Lord.  That was about 30 years ago when I was in college at PSU.  Lately, I've enjoyed reading "New Evidence that Demands a Verdict" by Josh McDowell.  He offers an excellent defense of the Bible and of the Gospels.

Even if you are not a Christian, I respect and applaud your courage to oppose the prevailing thought and think for yourself, based on what you observe around you.

Well, I look forward to reading your material on the Non-evolution site and also your books and/or writings on Ada!

P.S. "Back in the day" I used to work for Boeing and United Technologies on defense programs, i.e. aircraft.

Take care!

Eric

Evolutionists know that if you study the theory of evolution from a purely scientific viewpoint, you will see that science is against evolution. They fear that if you realize the theory of evolution is wrong, you will reject their secular humanist agenda, and will look for another explanation for life on this planet.

They fear that the other explanation will be Judaism, Christianity, or Islam.
